Estimated Sequencing Cost Analysis

US Market, Prices in USD (As of 2025)

Important Disclaimer

The following values are **order-of-magnitude estimates** for the US market and are intended to illustrate the cost relationships between different technologies and scenarios. Actual prices can vary significantly depending on the service provider, project scale, negotiated rates, and the reagents used. The listed costs typically cover **only the generation of sequencing data** and do not include the costs of library preparation, material isolation, or advanced bioinformatics analysis.

Transcriptomics (RNA) Scenarios

Isoform Discovery and Quantification PacBio Iso-Seq (Revio) 15 - 25M HiFi reads $800 - $1,300 Full-length transcripts. Requires RT-PCR (loss of RNA modifications).
