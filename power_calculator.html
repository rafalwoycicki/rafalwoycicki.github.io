Legend – Explanation of Variables

  • α (alpha): significance level – probability of false positive (e.g. 0.05)
  • β (beta): type II error rate – probability of false negative
  • Power: 1 − β – probability of correctly detecting a true difference
  • log2FC (Δ): log2 fold change in expression between groups
  • Dispersion: variability in expression between biological replicates
  • Reads per isoform: sequencing depth for each isoform/transcript
  • FDR: false discovery rate after multiple testing correction
