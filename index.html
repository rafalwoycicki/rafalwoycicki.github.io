Welcome to the Statistical Power Toolbox

This site provides two useful tools for estimating statistical power:

  • General Power Calculator: Estimate the power of a t-test based on significance level, sample size, mean difference, and standard deviation.
  • RNA-Seq Sample Size Estimator: Advanced tool to determine the required number of biological replicates in gene expression studies using short- or long-read RNA-Seq technologies (Illumina, PacBio, ONT). Supports isoform-level analysis and visualizes power vs sample size and read depth.
